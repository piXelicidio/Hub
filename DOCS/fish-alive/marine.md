## Marine demo scenes

### DemoBenchmark_Marine.unity

![Demo Scene](readme_media~/demoScene.jpg)

Benchmark scene for marine fish. Use the buttons to multiply fish and observe performance. The FPS is shown at the top left and fish count appears above the Reset button.

### Demo_Marine_CoralReef.unity

![Demo Pretty Coral Reef](readme_media~/demoPretty.jpg)

Full marine showcase with coral reef environment, helpers like `GroupOfFish`, effects, and interactions.

## Quick start (marine)

1. Drag a marine fish prefab into your scene.
2. Assign a target GameObject in the `FishMotion` component.
3. Press Play and move the target to see swimming behavior.

For scripts, behaviors, and shared setup, see `fishalive.md`.
